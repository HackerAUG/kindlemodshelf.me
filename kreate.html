Kreate

Kreate is a minimal drawing app for Kindle. Unleash your creativity and sketch directly on your e‑ink device using tap‑based modes—no drag tracking required. See the examples page to explore what others have created.

Challenges

Kindle’s kindle.gestures API recognizes taps and only coarse swipe directions (up, down, left, right), not precise finger paths or end points. This makes traditional “press‑and‑drag to draw” impossible.

To work around this, Kreate provides multiple drawing modes so you can choose exactly what to place—dots, lines, and even curves.

Kreate is designed to adapt to any Kindle screen size and model. If sizing looks off on your device, please report it so adjustments can be made.

Installation
  • Prerequisites: Jailbroken Kindle with Universal Hotfix and Winterbreak installed.
  • Copy the /kreate folder into /documents on your Kindle’s main storage (/mnt/us/documents from root).
  • That’s it—launch Kreate from your Kindle Home screen.
