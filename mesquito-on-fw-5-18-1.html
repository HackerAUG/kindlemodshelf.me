Mesquito on fw 5.18.1

I found a way to run Mesqutio on fw 5.18.1

What you do is: grab the release from https://github.com/HackerAUG/Run-Mesquito-on-firmware-5.18.1-and-possibly-higher-

 To install, you have to be jailbroken with koreader installed. download the zip and unpack everything to /mnt/us (The root of your kindle when connected to a computer.) Then, you open koreader terminal (more tools, terminal emulator) and run, mntroot rw, and then delete the folder "store" from /var/local/mesquite and "copy store_mesquito" and rename it "store" you have to use koreader to get to the root of your kindle, navigate to /var/local/mesquite and complete the above instructions. then MAKE SURE YOU RUN THIS: mntroot ro Hopefully it works for you guys. Tested onn kindle basic 11th gen 2024 release. Good Luck!
